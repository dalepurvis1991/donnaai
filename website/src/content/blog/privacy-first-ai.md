# Privacy-First AI: Our Commitment to Your Data

**Published:** December 5, 2024  
**Author:** Security Team

At Donna AI, we believe **privacy is a fundamental right**, not a feature. Here's how we protect your data.

## Zero-Data Retention

Your conversations and business data **never train our models**. Period.

When you interact with Donna:
- Queries are processed in real-time
- Results are returned immediately
- **Nothing is stored** after the session ends

## SOC2 Type II Compliance

We've completed our SOC2 Type II audit, demonstrating our commitment to:
- **Security**: Encrypted data in transit and at rest
- **Availability**: 99.9% uptime SLA
- **Confidentiality**: Role-based access controls
- **Privacy**: GDPR and CCPA compliant

## On-Premise Deployment

For organizations with strict data residency requirements, we offer:
- **Self-hosted instances** within your VPC
- **Air-gapped deployments** for maximum security
- **Custom SLAs** tailored to your compliance needs

## Transparent Security Practices

We publish:
- Regular security audits
- Penetration test results
- Incident response procedures
