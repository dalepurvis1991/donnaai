# Context is King: How Donna understands your work

**Published:** December 18, 2024  
**Author:** Engineering Team

They don't know that "The Smith Project" was renamed to "Project Falcon" last week. They don't know that you owe Sarah a budget report by EOD. They treat every interaction as Day 1.

## Privacy First

We know what you're thinking: "That sounds unsafe."

That's why we built Donna with a **privacy-first architecture**:
- Data stays in your secure instance (or local environment for Enterprise).
- We never train our public models on your private data.
- You control exactly which folders or timeframes are synced.

Context makes the assistant useful. Privacy makes it usable.
