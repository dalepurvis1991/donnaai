# How an Email Assistant Improves Client Relationships

**Published:** December 20, 2025  
**Author:** Donna AI Team

## Responsiveness is a competitive edge
Clients judge you by your communication. A delayed response can be perceived as neglect, while timely follow‑up builds trust. With high email volume, it’s easy to miss important messages.

## Consistent tone and professionalism
An AI assistant drafts replies using your past messages as a style guide. This ensures that your emails feel personal, not robotic.

## Remember every commitment
By extracting tasks and due dates, the assistant maintains a list of commitments—both those you owe and those you’re waiting on. It reminds you to send updates and check on deliverables.

## Build in personal touches
Your assistant can flag key personal details—birthdays, past projects, preferences—from previous conversations. When you follow up, you can reference these details, demonstrating genuine attention.
