Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data used to train your models? keyboard_arrow_down
Absolutely not. Donna AI operates on a strictly private architecture. Your data is processed in ephemeral instances and is never stored or used for model training unless you explicitly opt-in for a custom fine-tuned model on the Enterprise plan.
How long does the Pilot Program last? keyboard_arrow_down
The Pilot Program is designed as a 3-month validation period. After this period, you will have the option to transition to the Standard plan (with early-bird pricing lock) or move to an Enterprise contract depending on your usage volume.
Can I deploy Donna on my own servers? keyboard_arrow_down
Yes, on-premise deployment is available exclusively for Enterprise customers. We support deployments via Docker containers or Kubernetes clusters within your VPC (AWS, Azure, GCP).
What integrations are included in the Pilot? keyboard_arrow_down
The Pilot includes 5 standard integrations: Slack, Google Workspace (Gmail, Calendar, Drive), Microsoft 365, Notion, and Jira. Additional integrations can be requested but may require Enterprise access.
